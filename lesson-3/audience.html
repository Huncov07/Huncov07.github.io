Target Audience

rightweathersite.org is a website that can be used by anyone who is looking to find out more information about the weather in their area. It can be used by anyone who has mobile phones, tablets, and pcs. The people who will mainly be going to this site are people live in extreme cold weathers like Idaho and Utah, who need to constantly check the weather to know what kind of activities they will need to do that day and what kind of outfit they will need to wear. This site can also be used by anyone who wants to go on a vacation to another country and want to get a feel of what the weather is like there.

Two Persons

Person 1

Young people who enjoy activities outside. The average age is 20 years old. They usually access to internet on mobile devices and like to share photos on social networks. They need weather information to plan their outside activities like long walking on sunny days or snowboarding in the winter.

Person 2

Mature adults who need to keep informed in every moment, and wherever they are. They like to organize and plan their activities to be more productive. They need weather information to plan their meeting or just an informal lunch. They usually access to internet on their smartphones but also use laptops when they are working. Their average age is 35 years old.

Scenarios

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Why is rightweathersite.org important to me?

It is important to you because the site will help you get accurate weather information.

Scenario 2: Is there a preffered viewport that I should use when visiting rightweathersite?

There isn't. Our website has been designed to suite all posible users views. So, if you like using your phone, then it would work well for you.

Scenario 3: What are some of the design principles of rightweathersite?

We've applied some very important principles such as hick's law, fitt's law, rule of thirds, repitition in our shapes and much more!

Scenario 4: What are the main tenets of rightweathersite?

We belive that everyone should get the best and more accurate information a weather website can offer. So, we strive for perfection and customer happiness.

Scenario 5: What if rightweathersite crashes? How will you help?

For rightweathersite to crash, it would mean that the internet crashes. We have dozens of server all constantly running and ready to go if one fails. We also have more than one satalite gathering information for our site.
